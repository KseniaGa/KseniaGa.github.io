About Me

I am Kseniia Harshina, currently a University Assistant at the University of Klagenfurt, Austria. My research focuses on developing a Virtual-Reality Game for Empathy Enhancement and Perspective-Taking in the Context of Refugee Experiences. I have a background in game studies and engineering, with experience in machine learning, swarm intelligence, and game design.

I completed my M.Sc. in Game Studies and Engineering from Alpen-Adria University, Klagenfurt, where my master's thesis explored generating UAV countermeasures using machine learning. I hold a B.Sc. in Software Engineering from the National Technical University of Ukraine 'Igor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute.'
